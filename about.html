Welcome to

Symbol Advertisement

Established nearly 20 years ago by Abdul Letheif, Symbol Advertisement is a trusted agency known for its reliable, no-nonsense approach. We specialize in manpower solutions, board works, flux works, and practical advertising support for businesses that value experience and results.

Proudly serving our clients since 2005

At Symbol Advertisement, we believe in getting the job done right. Our agency is built on years of hands-on experience in manpower supply, board works, and flux works for a wide range of businesses. We focus on practical solutions and dependable service, not flashy trends.

Led by Abdul Letheif, our team understands the value of hard work and straightforward communication. We’ve supported factories, shops, and companies with reliable manpower and board work services for nearly two decades. Our approach is honest, efficient, and grounded in real-world results.

While we have a creative team available to support branding and advertising needs, our main strength lies in our traditional values and commitment to our clients’ success. If you need a partner who values experience, reliability, and long-term relationships, Symbol Advertisement is here for you.
